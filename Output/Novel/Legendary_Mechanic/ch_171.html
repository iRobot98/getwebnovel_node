Chapter 171: Trading Storm (2)

Translator: Atlas Studios  Editor: Atlas Studios

After a few minutes of waiting, a man wearing a pair of sunglasses, a hat, a dark green coat and a scarf covering the lower half of his face came into the cafe and sat opposite Han Xiao. He placed the metal suitcase he was carrying on the table, looked at Han Xiao and didn’t say a word.

The seller knew the buyer was Black Phantom, but Han Xiao was using a different face.

Han Xiao and the seller stared at each other for a while and confirmed the other party’s identity in silence – strangers wouldn’t have stared at each other for this long without saying a word. The seller entered the passcode and faced the open suitcase towards Han Xiao.

Inside the suitcase was a doughnut-shaped small device with its wires exposed outside. The exterior was made in metal, with an intruding cylinder in the cross section and dim red lights flashing through the gaps. This was the Mini Bonfire Reaction Furnace, an energy core that is able to generate electricity automatically from solar and condensed fuel. It was also a piece of superhuman equipment as energy could be turned into fuel as a power source.

A rechargeable battery is a core material for mechanical suits, it eliminates the need and trouble of battery supplies, imagine having to change batteries in the middle of a fight.

Han Xiao confirmed it was the real thing with his hands, took out his laptop, sent the money to the seller in a flash and immediately said: “Run!”

Without waiting for the seller’s reaction, Han Xiao stored Mini Bonfire Reaction Furnace into his backpack and stood up suddenly, he took out his Berserk Eagle and shot many times at the people he previously confirmed to be monitoring him.

Ordina agents took action as soon as the deal was completed, but Han Xiao was a second quicker, he had the upper hand!

The firepower at within ten meters from a gun with such high calibre was as high as a sniper, all the monitoring agents who got shot flew backwards.

Although the agents were well trained and wore a bulletproof vest, they were just normal soldiers. However, Han Xiao was a level 55 Mechanic, his guns were strengthened and he had all kinds of skills that further increases their power, one shot was enough to make the agents lose their fighting ability and put them in a critically injured state.

Without any hesitation, the seller broke through the floor-to-ceiling window and ran with all his might. He was a superhuman too, as he covered a few meters each step he took.

To divert the numbers of the enemy, Han Xiao ran towards another direction.

“Damn it, who is this buyer!” The Ordina mission commander burst into a rage as he did not expect the buyer to be strong enough to realise the danger beforehand, and this had caused the plan to be interrupted. He ordered the agents and the soldiers to split up to chase them separately.

Han Xiao spat water across the streets as he dashed across the puddles with high speed.

As countless footsteps closed in on Han Xiao, agents appeared from the streets around and started shooting at him. The people on the street ran away screaming.

The magnetically-controlled retractable armor Han Xiao just equipped reflected the bullets away, he slid across the battlefield with a gun in both hands and shot at any agent who exposed their head, he didn’t miss a single shot.

Suddenly, Han Xiao felt a pull on his feet, he looked down and saw the water-sediment wrapping around his calf, slowing down his speed.

“superhuman agents of Ordina has stricken.” Han Xiao saw the information of the enemy from the interface, an Esper around level 30.

As Han Xiao’s speed slowed down, two Pugilist agents used the opportunity to dash out from the roadside and started attacking him with energy covered fists and legs.

As the enemies closed in, Han Xiao remained calm and used Sunworm’s [Concentrated Spray]. The orange colored condense flames devoured the two Pugilist, the continuous damage was very high. They let out a scream and backed away immediately.

The flames vapourised the rain and created a white fog, which obstructed the view of the agents.

Han Xiao has no intention to fight in Ordina against their own agents, he tore apart the water wrapped around his calves by turning his Electromagnetic Skateboard Shoes to full capacity and backed away from the enemy Esper controlled area as quickly as he could.

It was at this moment when he heard the sound of engines as two enormous black police vehicles – the size of an RV 1 , dashed out from the intersection. The vehicles splashed the water in an arc with a drift and stopped in front of Han Xiao, facing him sideways and blocked the path.

High caliber guns extended out from inside the windows as they rolled down and shot Han Xiao at the same time, the firepower was so strong that it created sparkles out of the muzzle for an astonishing half meter.

As the bullets expanded in his sight, Han Xiao got hit head-on. These guns were high caliber assault rifles, and some of them were shotguns which had knock-back effects. Although he had armor on, he still stumbled from the bullets.

Information of the enemies appeared on the interface, they were Ordina’s Special Operation Unit.

Han Xiao made a sharp change in direction and slid across the buildings through roofs of the two RV sized vehicles with his Electromagnetic Skateboard Shoes. The soldiers in the vehicles alighted hastily to fire at him, but the bullets couldn’t catch up to him and left a trail of holes on the walls of the building. Han Xiao took out a sonic bomb, pulled out the safe with his teeth and threw it into the middle of the RV.

The soldiers split out hurriedly trying to find cover, but the sound waves from the sonic boom were not obstructed by their cover, it spread out and the entire special units fainted immediately. Han Xiao burst their tires and left in a stroll.

Ordina has a lot of men around this area. No matter how Han Xiao tried to get rid of the pursuit, there will always be another car at the front of his path. He shot down the traffic cams, took detours, used his Electromagnetic Skateboard Shoes to climb over walls and buildings, outran the soldiers time and time again, but there will always be cars stopping him in the front.

“How are they able to always find me?” Han Xiao thought, then suddenly, he realised something. As he turned back to look up the sky while sliding, he discovered three drones that were following him, no wonder they knew where he was. He took out Red Falcon and shot them down with one bullet each.

Ordina’s mission commander bashed the table in anger, the other team had already lost the seller, Han Xiao was the only target left but he was too difficult for his men to catch.

“Mag, take him down!”

…

Having successfully escaped from the last few cars, Han Xiao jumped across the gaps between buildings and looked for a hidden place to change his face and clothes. Suddenly, bullets from a shotgun exploded on his back, smoke and flames broke out.

Han Xiao got knocked straight into the wall, lime and broken bricks broke fell onto the floor.

Close range explosion had made Han Xiao lose his hearing for a little, he got rid of the lime on his head and looked up with a frown. A man was standing at the top of a building, he had all kinds of equipment on him — a bulletproof vest, waterproof clip belts, 4 handguns around his waist and thighs, a sniper rifle and two different assault rifles hanging behind his back, and a shotgun in his hands with smoke coming out of the muzzle.

The damage from one shot exceeded the damage of common firearms, Han Xiao looked at the interface and surprise appeared on his face.

“a CannonMaster?”
